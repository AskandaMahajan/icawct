ABOUT THE CONFERENCE ICAWCT-2024

Scope of the Conference

The "Advanced Wireless Communication Technology" conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and experts from around the world to explore the latest developments, innovations, and challenges in the field of wireless communication. This conference provides a unique platform for sharing knowledge, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaborations to advance the state-of-the-art in wireless communication technology.

The conference scope encompasses a wide range of topics related to advanced wireless communication, including but not limited to:

  • 5G and Beyond: Advancements and applications of fifth-generation (5G) and future generations of wireless communication technologies, such as 6G and beyond.
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Wireless Sensor Networks: Integration of IoT devices and wireless sensor networks into communication infrastructures for smart applications and industrial automation.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Wireless Communication: Leveraging AI and machine learning techniques to enhance network performance, optimization, and resource management.
  • Spectrum Management and Efficiency: Strategies for efficient spectrum utilization, spectrum sharing, and cognitive radio technologies.
  • Secure and Reliable Communication: Advances in wireless security, privacy preservation, and protection against cyber threats in wireless networks.
  • Edge Computing and Fog Networking: Exploiting edge and fog computing paradigms to reduce latency and enhance data processing in wireless networks.
  • Wireless Communication for Autonomous Systems: Wireless technologies enabling communication in autonomous vehicles, unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), and robotics.
  • Massive MIMO and Beamforming: Research on massive multiple-input, multiple-output (MIMO) and beamforming techniques for increased capacity and coverage.
  • Next-Generation Satellite Communication: Innovations in satellite communication systems and their integration with terrestrial networks.
  • Green Wireless Communication: Energy-efficient solutions and sustainable practices for wireless communication to reduce environmental impact.
  • Millimeter-Wave and Terahertz Communication: Exploring the potential of millimeter-wave and terahertz frequencies for high-speed wireless communication.
  • Wireless Health Monitoring and Biomedical Applications: Wireless technologies for remote health monitoring, medical imaging, and telemedicine applications.

The conference invites original research papers, technical presentations, posters, and demonstrations on these topics and related areas. Submissions are expected to present novel findings, practical implementations, theoretical advancements, and case studies that contribute significantly to the advancement of wireless communication technology.
Furthermore, the conference encourages interdisciplinary discussions, welcoming contributions that explore the intersection of wireless communication with other fields, such as artificial intelligence, big data analytics, smart cities, and the Internet of Things.
The "Advanced Wireless Communication Technology" conference seeks to foster an environment of collaboration, networking, and knowledge exchange among academia, industry, and policymakers. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in lively discussions, attend insightful keynote talks, and explore cutting-edge technologies showcased by industry exhibitors.
